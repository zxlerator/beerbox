Stay in the moment, not the line

Hate lines and love beer? Us too.

That’s why we created BeerBox, which is a super cool vending machine that dispenses cold beer cans at music festivals, concerts, and sporting events. It’s easy to use and fast! Cheers to no more lines.


Fast

It works like magic and elevates the beer can out of the machine. It even opens the can for you, too!

Easy

Just swipe your credit card or tap your phone with Apple Pay to get your beer. The days of having your crumpled dollar rejected are over.

Amazing

It’s the first time you’ll ever want to take a selfie with a vending machine. We guarantee a 543% increase in your Instagram likes.
