More About Us

BeerBox is the first IoT vending device that cools, accepts payment, and opens a can of beer at entertainment-led and outdoor occasions. Long lines at beer concessions are unfortunately an expected occurrence for attendees at music festivals, concerts, and sporting events. BeerBox adds more distribution points within a venue to reduce long lines, create a new sales channel for beer, and increases incremental sales.
